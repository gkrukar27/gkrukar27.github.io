Safe Products

Many products that people generally use are actually harmful to people with eczema. It is best to use products that are unscented and made with as little ingredients as possible. For people with Contact Dermatitis, this is especially important. Contact with one allergen can cause a massive flare up, so here I list some common brands that are safe for me, and likely for most people with moderate-severe eczema. I recommend adhering to the name that appears on the list, sometimes they can trick you!
